Aquaponics open-source project

On 2018 through our startups incubator we helped developed this aquaponics Deep Water Culture project called "Aquaponia Tapatia" in Guadalajara Mexico, which is now one of the major producers of Tilapia and French heirloom hidroponic lettuce in the city. This project is resource efficient because it uses limited amounts of water that is constantly recycled, also all sensors used in the greenhouse were programmed with open-source code.

Biogas production project

In conjunction with the federal government through it's Agriculture Department and it's Trust Fund for Shared Risk, AgroFuture Consulting and Lacteos La Villa developed this biodigestor project in Coahuila, Mexico. Nowadays this enterprise is an environmentally frienly role model in the region.
